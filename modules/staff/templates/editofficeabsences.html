This page is for marking multiple employee absences on the same day. This is handy for offices with large number of employees who might have short (preferably one day) absences.

For other cases, editing absence calendar for each employee might be much more effective. Choosing which form to use, please consider that submitting form on this page will create separate ticket for each person-day combination, and this will mean more work to line managers on accepting the requests.
